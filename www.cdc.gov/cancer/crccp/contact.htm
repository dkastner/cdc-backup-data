Programs That Offer Screening Services

You may be eligible for free or low-cost screenings if you meet these qualifications—

  • You are between 50 and 64 years of age.
  • You are at average risk for colorectal cancer.
  • You have no insurance, or your insurance does not cover screening exams.
  • Your yearly income is at or below 250% of the federal poverty level.

The following six programs provide colorectal cancer screening services to eligible men and women as recommended by the U.S. Preventive Services Task Force—
