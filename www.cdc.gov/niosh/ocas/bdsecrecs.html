Advisory Board SEC Petition Recommendations

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

The Advisory Board provides advice to the Secretary of the Department of Health and Human Services on whether there is a class of employees who should be added to the SEC. The Advisory Board's recommendation is based on:

  • Whether the class of employees was exposed to radiation and it is not feasible to estimate their radiation dose.
  • Whether there is reasonable likelihood that such radiation doses may have endangered the health of members of the class.

Locate Advisory Board Recommendations on SEC Petitions Alphabetically

Do you know what Advisory Board SEC recommendation you are looking for? If you do, click on the letter below that corresponds to the first letter of the facility/site that the recommendation covers.
