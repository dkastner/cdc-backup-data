2011 NPPTL Sponsored Meetings

September 14, 2011
Respirator Manufacturers Meeting
The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH), National Personal Protective Technology Laboratory (NPPTL), Technology Evaluation Branch (TEB) is hosting a meeting for all respirator manufacturers. All manufacturers, manufacturer application preparers, and other interested parties are welcome to attend. The meeting will be held at the Wildwood Lodge, 8511 Hudson Blvd., Lake Elmo, Minnesota.

July 26 - 28, 2011
Respirator Manufacturers Meeting
The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH), National Personal Protective Technology Laboratory (NPPTL) and the Health and Safety Laboratory (HSL) are co-hosting a meeting for all respirator manufacturers. The meeting will be held from July 26 through July 28, 2011, and will be held at the Health and Safety Laboratory in Buxton Derbyshire, United Kingdom . All manufacturers and other interested parties are welcome to attend.

March 29, 2011
Information Update for the PPT Program Stakeholder Meeting, March 29, 2011
The NIOSH Personal Protective Technology Program held its fourth annual Stakeholder Meeting on March 29, 2011 at the Hyatt Regency Pittsburgh Airport. Four industry sectors were emphasized at this meeting: Healthcare, Public Safety, Agriculture, and Mining and included breakout sessions focused on these sectors. CM credits were awarded through the ABIH for certified industrial hygienists attending the Stakeholders meeting. View the Presentations and Posters from the Stakeholders Meeting and Workshops.

2010 NPPTL Sponsored Meetings

December 9, 2010
Public Meeting
NIOSH held a public meeting concerning (1) the NIOSH Regulatory Agenda for updating 42 CFR Part 84, (2) Standards Development for CBRN Combination Unit Respirators (Any combination of an air-purifying and air-supplied respirator), and (3) Standards Development for Buddy Breathing with a Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA).

August 24 – 26, 2010
Respirator Manufacturers Meeting
NIOSH/NPPTL and China Medical University, Taiwan co-hosted a meeting for respirator manufacturers and other interested parties at the China Medical University in Taichung, Taiwan.

July 29, 2010
Public Meeting
NIOSH held a second public meeting concerning the proposed rule for Total Inward Leakage Requirements for Respirators. The purpose of the meeting was to allow participants to make presentations to NIOSH, share results of any new research that may be available or in process in the area of filtering facepiece or other half-mask respirator inward leakage measurement, and offer any additional comments on the anticipated economic impact of the proposed rule.

July 14, 2010
Respirator Manufacturers Meeting

March 2 – 3, 2010
PPT Program Stakeholders Meeting
Aspects of the PPT program, including what has been done, what is currently being done, and what needs to be done will be discussed. The meeting presentations and breakout sessions are available for viewing.

May 12, 2009
Respirator Manufacturers Meeting
The meeting presentations are available for viewing.

March 03, 2009
PPT Stakeholder Meeting
