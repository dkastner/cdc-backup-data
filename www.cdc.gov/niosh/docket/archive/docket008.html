Powered Air-Purifying respirators (multifunction)

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir
March 2003
NIOSH Docket Number 008

This docket collects public comments related to the development of new, comprehensive standards for certifying multi-function PAPRs (powered air-purifying respirators). Information submitted to the docket will be considered in the context of development of conceptual standards for PAPRs, as well as updating equipment performance requirements to improve respirator user safety and health.
