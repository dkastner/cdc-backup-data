Course Audit and Renewals

NIOSH Approved Spirometry Training Program Course Audit and Sponsorship Renewal Due Dates

This page is intended for use by sponsors who provide initial and refresher spirometry courses as part of the NIOSH-Approved Spirometry Training Program. Listed below are the sponsors whose curriculums have been evaluated by NIOSH and meet the minimum requirements for spirometry training established by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration and NIOSH. The listing includes the NIOSH-Approved Sponsor Number, Sponsor Name, Date of the Last Course Audit, and the Dates on which the Sponsorship (including the initial course) and Refresher Course renewals are required.

If you wish to contact an individual sponsor, please click on the sponsor name and you will be redirected to another NIOSH web page with specific contact information.
