Noteworthy Publications

Dust Control Handbook for Industrial Minerals Mining and Processing

Mined ore undergoes crushing, grinding, cleaning, drying, and product sizing as it is processed, generating harmful dust. This handbook reviews technologies for lowering dust levels below the permissible or recommended occupational exposure limits.

Practical Demonstrations of Ergonomic Principles

This document consists of a series of demonstrations designed to complement training on ergonomic principles.

Reducing Low Back Pain and Disability in Mining

This report provides managers, supervisors, and safety and health professionals with information on low back pain (LBP) and low back disability. It also provides the latest LBP research and describes methods to address the problem.
