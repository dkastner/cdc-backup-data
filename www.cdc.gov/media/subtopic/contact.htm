CDC’s Main Press Line

  • Media Request Form
  • e-mail the Newsroom
  • Phone: (404) 639-3286
  • Fax: (404)718-1630

Thank you for your interest in public health and the work CDC does. If you need information or would like to request an interview, please fill out and submit the Media Request Form. If you work for a media outlet and are trying to reach CDC’s Media Office after 6:00 PM about a public health emergency, please call (770) 488-7100. All non-emergency after-hours requests will be processed between 9:00 a.m. and 6:00 p.m. ET Monday through Friday.

All other non-media seeking information from CDC, please call 1-800-CDC-INFO (800-232-4636) TTY: (888) 232-6348 or send a message to CDC-INFO.
