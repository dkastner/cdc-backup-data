Morbidity and Mortality Weekly

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Published every Thursday, the Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report is CDC's weekly journal highlighting public health investigation, along with providing reports and recommendations derived from science-based research. The Division of Media Relations puts together press summary packets each week, including contact information for each article. To receive these press summaries on an embargoed basis, send an e-mail to media@cdc.gov. Please note that this e-mail list is for credentialed journalists only. For all others, please visit Hookup to Health to sign up for e-mail updates.
