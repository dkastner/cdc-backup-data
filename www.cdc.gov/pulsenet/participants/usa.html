PulseNet USA Participants

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

PulseNet USA consists of over 83 laboratories in seven regions of the United States. PulseNet USA headquarters are located at the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention in Atlanta, Georgia. PulseNet USA works with PulseNet International by supporting and participating in strategic planning meetings, training international participants at PulseNet USA headquarters, and sharing protocols and software customization. Click on each state to find the public health laboratory that is part of PulseNet’s national network.
