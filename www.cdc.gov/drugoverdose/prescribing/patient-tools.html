Guideline Resources: Patient & Partner Tools

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Prescription opioids can be used to help treat moderate-to-severe pain and but can also have serious risks and side effects. It is essential that you and your doctor discuss treatment options with all of the risks and benefits carefully considered. Below are some patient and partner-specific tools to make sure everyone has the information they need.
