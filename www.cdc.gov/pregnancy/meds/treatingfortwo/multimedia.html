Buttons

CDC has created buttons to encourage healthcare providers and women to discuss the risks and benefits of medication use before and during pregnancy. You can help by posting a button to your website, blog, or social networking site (e.g., Facebook page). To add the desired button to your site: select a size below and click the link, copy the code and then paste to your site.
