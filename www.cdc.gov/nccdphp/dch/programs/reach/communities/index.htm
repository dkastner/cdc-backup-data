What are REACH communities doing?

REACH Communities

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

CDC’s REACH program funds 40 grantee partners through REACH U.S. and 10 grantee partners through REACH CORE. See the map for grantee locations.

Grantees focus on improving health and ending health gaps among these racial and ethnic groups:

  • African American/Black.
  • American Indian/Alaska Native.
  • Asian.
  • Native Hawaiian/Other Pacific Islander.
  • Hispanic/Latino.

See a list of REACH grantees listed by racial and ethnic group.

REACH-funded partners focus on one or more of these health priority area(s):

  • Breast and cervical cancer.
  • Heart disease.
  • Diabetes.
  • Adult and older adult immunization (vaccines).
  • Hepatitis B.
  • Tuberculosis.
  • Asthma.
  • Infant mortality.

See a list of REACH grantees listed by health priority areas.
