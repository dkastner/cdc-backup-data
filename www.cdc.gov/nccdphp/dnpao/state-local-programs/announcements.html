Vital Signs: Hospital Support for Breastfeeding
Hospital practices in the first hours and days after birth make the difference in whether and how long babies are breastfed.

Step it Up! The Surgeon General’s Call to Action to Promote Walking and Walkable Communities
This report presents five goals and supporting implementation strategies to help Americans be physically active and for the nation to better support walking and walkable communities for people of all ages and abilities.
