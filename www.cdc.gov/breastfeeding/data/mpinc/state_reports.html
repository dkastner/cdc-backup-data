mPINC State Reports

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Aggregate data are shared with state-level organizations from state health departments to state breastfeeding coalition to facilitate their work with hospitals and birth centers in improving maternity care. State Reports summarize each state’s facilities’ strengths in breastfeeding support as well as the areas in need of improvement. These reports identify opportunities for states to better protect, promote, and support breastfeeding mothers and infants, and reports from multiple years allow for tracking trends over time.
