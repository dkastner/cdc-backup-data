Español: Materiales para hispanohablantes

These Spanish-language materials focus on whooping cough (pertussis) symptoms, diagnosis, and prevention.

Fact Sheets (Hojas informativas)

You Can Start Protecting Your Baby from Whooping Cough before Birth [1.30 MB, 2 pages]
(Puedes empezar a proteger a tu bebé de la tosferina desde antes del nacimiento)
This fact sheet explains that when pregnant women get the whooping cough vaccine during their third trimester, they will pass antibodies to their babies before birth. This allows babies to be born with protection against whooping cough.

  • Order free small quantities (CDC-INFO product #221895)

 

Tdap Vaccine for Preteens and Teens: Information for Parents [1 page]
(Información para los padres: La vacuna Tdap para preadolescentes y adolescentes)
This 8-1/2" x 11" fact sheet answers general questions about the booster shot that protects preteens and teens.

 

Whooping Cough for Parents: The Basics [1 page]
(Información para los padres: Tosferina (pertussis))
This 8-1/2" x 11" fact sheet describes symptoms of infection as well as benefits and risks of vaccination.

 

Whooping Cough for Parents: In Depth
(Tosferina (pertussis) para los padres: Informe detallado)
This 8-1/2" x 11" fact sheet offers a story from a family affected by whooping cough. It also describes symptoms of infection, benefits and risks of vaccination, details about the vaccine, and history of the disease.

Matte Releases (or Formatted Articles) (Comunicado de prensa (o artículos listos para publicación))

Grandparents Can Help Protect Against Whooping Cough with Tdap Vaccine [1 page] Updated 1/10/2014
(Los abuelos pueden ayudar a proteger a sus nietos contra la tosferina con la vacuna Tdap)
Babies who get whooping cough often catch it from family members, including grandparents, who may not even know they have whooping cough. Before you cuddle with your new, little bundle, it's important to get a Tdap vaccine.

 

Whooping Cough Cases Are on the Rise – Be Protected [1 page] Updated 9/2014
(Los casos de la tosferina están aumentando ¿Está protegido?)
The United States is experiencing an increase in whooping cough. Protect yourself and protect vulnerable infants — get vaccinated.

Podcasts

Born with Protection against Whooping Cough (3:14 minutes, Date Released: 4/13/2015)
(Haz que tu bebé nazca protegido contra la tosferina)
This podcast provides information about whooping cough, a disease that can be deadly for babies, and CDC’s recommendation that all women receive the Tdap vaccine during the third trimester of every pregnancy so their babies can be born with protection from this serious disease.

Whooping Cough Vaccine Recommendation for Babies and Young Children (4:02 minutes, Date Released: 4/13/2015)
(Recomendación sobre la vacuna contra la tosferina para los bebés y niños pequeños)
This podcast provides information about whooping cough and the recommendation that all children receive the DTaP vaccine, according to CDC’s recommended schedule, to help protect them from this serious disease.

Whooping Cough Vaccine Recommendation for Preteens and Teens (3:23 minutes, Date Released: 4/13/2015)
(Recomendación sobre la vacuna contra la tosferina para los preadolescentes y adolescents)
This podcast provides information about whooping cough and the recommendation that all preteens receive the Tdap vaccine when they are 11 or 12 to help protect them from this serious disease.

Recognizing and Preventing Whooping Cough (Pertussis) (3:24 minutes, Date Released: 4/13/2015)
(Reconocimiento y prevención de la tosferina)
This podcast provides information about the symptoms of whooping cough and how vaccines can help prevent this serious disease for people of all ages. It is especially important for those who will have close contact with a baby to be up to date with their whooping cough vaccine.

Whooping Cough Public Service Announcement (0:30 minutes, Date Released: 4/13/2015)
(Anuncio de servicio público sobre la tosferina)
This 30 second PSA encourages pregnant women to get the whooping cough vaccine, called Tdap, during the third trimester of each pregnancy in order to pass antibodies to their babies so they are born with protection against this serious disease.

Pregnant? Get Vaccinated.
(¿Embarazada? Vacúnese.)
Learn more about how to protect your new baby and the rest of your family from whooping cough.

 

Circle of Protection
(Círculo de Protección)
Don’t risk spreading whooping cough to your baby. Get vaccinated to form a circle of protection. Protect your baby by getting vaccinated yourself and making sure others around your baby get their Tdap booster vaccine against whooping cough.

Web Features (Especiales en el sitio web)

Are You Pregnant? Get Tdap during Your Third Trimester
(¿Está embarazada? Reciba la Tdap en su tercer trimester)
Only you can give your baby protection against whooping cough before your little one is born. Talk to your doctor or midwife so that he/she will give you the Tdap vaccine during your third trimester.

 

Protect Babies against Whooping Cough
(Proteja a los bebés contra la tosferina (pertussis))
Whooping cough is very contagious and can make people very sick, especially babies that are too young to have received all of their own vaccines. Protect babies against whooping cough by getting vaccinated and making sure your child gets all of his/her vaccines.
