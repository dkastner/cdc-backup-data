Virtual Event 12-08-2012

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

The Electronic Health Records Meaningful Use & Public Health Virtual Event took place on Tuesday, December 18th from 12:00 – 5:30pm (EST). Event recording and transcription will be made available after the holidays, please check back here for updates. Can’t wait that long? Check out the slides from all the presentations, which are now accessible from our virtual event page. On behalf of CDC, we thank all our presenters for sharing valuable knowledge with us and our audience for joining us and making it a successful event.
