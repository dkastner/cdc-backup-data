Act Against AIDS

Language:
English
  • Español (Spanish)
Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir
 


Condoms are highly effective at preventing both HIV and other STDs.


Pre-exposure prophylaxis (PrEP) can greatly reduce your risk of HIV.


Knowing your HIV status helps you choose options to stay healthy.


HIV Treatment keeps you healthy and protects others.

Start Talking. Stop HIV. is a campaign that seeks to reduce HIV infections among gay, bisexual, and other men who have sex with men by encouraging open discussion between sex partners and friends about a range of HIV prevention strategies.
