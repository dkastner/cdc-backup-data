CDC Workshop for Risk-based Funding

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Select the video to watch

  • Opening Plenary-Public Health Emergency Preparedness: Budget Period 11 Risk-based Funding Pilot Project presented at the CDC Workshop with Risk-based Funding Pilot Sites
    September 29, 2011

    Christine Kosmos, RN, BSN, MS, Division Director, Division of State and Local Readiness, CDC

    This session will provide an overview of the workshop agenda and expected outcomes as well as review and discussion of the risk-based funding pilot project goal and objectives.

  • BP 11 Application Discussion Current State and Way Forward presented at the CDC Workshop with Risk-based Funding Pilot Sites
    September 29, 2011
     

    • Christine Kosmos, RN, BSN, MS, Division Director, Division of State and Local Readiness, CDC
    • Christa-Marie Singleton, MD, MPH, Associate Director for Science, Division of State and Local Readiness, CDC
    • Jeff Bryant, MS, MA, Program Services Branch Chief, Division of State and Local Readiness, CDC

    This session will provide an overview of the PHEP BP 11 application results as well as an overview of the expectation for possible risk-based funding pilot project activities and how these activities are designed to address how public health, medical, or mental/behavioral risk mitigation efforts are addressed.

  • Community Engagement Leveraging Other Federal Programs presented at the CDC Workshop with Risk-based Funding Pilot Sites
    September 29, 2011

    Sharon Ricks, MA, Deputy Regional Health Administrator, US. Department of Health and Human Services

    This interactive session will provide insight on strategies for leveraging other federal programs in your community engagement efforts.
