Four Major Initiatives

Standardizing Health Data and Exchange Systems

The Nationally Notifiable Disease Surveillance System (NNDSS) Modernization Initiative (NMI) is underway to enhance the system’s surveillance capabilities to provide more comprehensive, timely, and higher quality data than ever before for public health decision making.

More

Enhancing Electronic Health Record Systems

BioSense is a syndromic public health surveillance system designed to help health officials at the state, tribal, local, and territorial levels. Its purpose is to efficiently, rapidly and collaboratively monitor and respond to harmful health effects of exposure to diseases or hazardous conditions.

More

Accelerating Electronic Laboratory Reporting (ELR)

Electronic Laboratory Reporting (ELR) is the electronic transmission from laboratories to public health of laboratory reports which identify reportable conditions. ELR has many benefits, including improved timeliness, reduction of manual data entry errors, and reports that are more complete.

More

Modernizing Mortality Surveillance Systems

The National Vital Statistics System (NVSS) is the nation’s birth, marriage, divorce, and death registry system. The NVSS initiative will transition the current system to electronic reporting to produce more rapid mortality reporting.

More

 Additional Focus Areas

  • NNDSS Modernization Initiative (NMI)

    An initiative to modernize the systems and processes used to receive nationally notifiable disease data to provide more comprehensive, timely, and higher quality data than ever before for public health decision making.

    More  

  • CHIIC

    The CDC Health Information Innovation Consortium (CHIIC) serves as a channel for innovative projects in CDC programs and collaborations with State, Tribal, Local and Territorial (STLT) agencies with the goal to encourage innovative projects with Public Health Informatics and Health IT activities across the agency.

    More  

  • Vital Statistics Rapid Release

    The new Vital Statistics Rapid Release (VSRR) program provides access to the most timely vital statistics for public health surveillance, through 1) pilot releases of Quarterly Provisional Estimates and 2) Special Reports based on a current flow of vital statistics data from state vital records offices.

    More
