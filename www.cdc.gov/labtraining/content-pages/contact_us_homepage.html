    Web Site Technical Issues

    For Web site technical issues (i.e. dead links or a page doesn’t load) please send a message to the labtraining@cdc.gov address. If you have non-technical questions, concerns or suggestions please contact us using the form below.

     

    Report an Accessibility Problem

    If you would like to report an accessibility problem with the CDC Web site, please visit http://www.cdc.gov/accessibility.

     

    Please Note:

    • CDC's website provides health information for your general knowledge.
    • Concerns about a medical condition, either your own or that of a family member, should always be addressed to your primary health care professional for advice and care appropriate to your specific medical needs.
    • CDC does not recommend self-diagnosis or self-management of health problems that should be attended to by health care professionals.
