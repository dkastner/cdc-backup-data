PRAMStat System

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

PRAMStat is an online data platform developed to provide public access to over 250 maternal and child health indicators from the Pregnancy Risk Assessment Monitoring System (PRAMS). Users can view and interact with PRAMS visualizations, such as maps and charts, and save visualizations for use in creating fact sheets and presentations.  Use PRAMStat to explore PRAMS data from 2000 through 2011 from a single state or select a topic and compare data across states.  Learn More .
