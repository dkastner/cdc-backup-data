Participating PRAMS States, Territory and Tribe

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Forty-seven states, New York City, Puerto Rico, the District of Columbia and the Great Plains Tribal Chairmen’s Health Board (GPTCHB) currently participate in PRAMS, representing approximately 83% of all U.S. live births. Two other states (California and Ohio) previously participated. Click on the map or the name of the state to see state information or expand and/or download the complete data table below the map.

Note: Participating but not included in the above map or expandable data table:

GPTCHB http://gptchb.org/
