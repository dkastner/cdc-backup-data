Health-Related Quality of Life (HRQOL) Data

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Health-related quality of life is an individual's or a group's perceived physical and mental health over time.  This site provides public access to eight different health-related quality of life measures yearly from 1993 through 2010 for the entire United States and individual U.S. States overall, by gender, by age group, and by race/ethnicity.

The health-related quality of life website provides access to general information about the program and its history, background on health-related quality of life, and publications validating and analyzing measures.
