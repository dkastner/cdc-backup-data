TAP CLABSI Implementation Guide - Links to Example Resources

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Central Line-Associated Bloodstream Infections (CLABSI) Implementation Guide: Links to Resources*

*Disclaimer: The links in the domains below are not mutually exclusive nor do they represent an exhaustive list of all the possible resources available. Furthermore, the links presented do not constitute an endorsement of these organizations or their programs by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) or the federal government, and none should be inferred.

See also the CDC 2011 Guidelines for the Prevention of Intravascular Catheter-Related Infections [PDF - 1 MB]

I. General Infrastructure, Capacity, and Processes

Engagement of Leadership, Champions, and Healthcare Personnel

  • Comprehensive Unit-based Safety Program (CUSP) Board Checklist [PDF - 589 KB]
    Checklist of CUSP activities for board members and senior leaders to prevent healthcare-associated infections (HAI), from the Armstrong Institute for Patient Safety and Quality at Johns Hopkins Medicine
  • Engage the Senior Executive Module - CUSP Toolkit
    Module with multiple tools for engagement of senior executives in CUSP activities, from the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ)
  • Accountability of Health Care Personnel in Preventing CLABSIs [PDF - 81 KB]
    Framework of personnel accountability and responsibilities in prevention of CLABSIs, from The Joint Commission
  • Back to Basics: 12 Tenets to Reduce CLABSIs
    List of 12 tenets of collaboration to reduce CLABSIs, from the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ)
  • Steps in Developing a Business Case Analysis [PDF - 38 KB]
    A framework listing nine steps that healthcare personnel can use to build a business case for CLABSI prevention, from The Joint Commission
  • HAI Cost Calculator
    Downloadable Excel files that allow hospitals to calculate facility specific costs of healthcare-associated infections and savings realized by preventing them, from the Association for Professionals in Infection Control and Epidemiology (APIC)
  • Individual Self-Assessment for CLABSI Champions [PDF - KB]
    Self-assessment for individuals who may champion CLABSI prevention efforts to ensure foundational knowledge with links to many tools, from The Joint Commission
  • Comprehensive Unit-based Safety Program (CUSP) Tools
    Tools to Engage, Educate, Execute, and Evaluate CLABSI prevention efforts, from the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ)
  • Lessons Learned from Keystone ICU Project [PDF - 68 KB]
    Guidance for large-scale improvement projects based on lessons learned from a successful ICU collaborative to reduce CLABSI rates, from The Joint Commission

Tools for the CLABSI Workgroup

  • APIC Implementation Guide to Preventing Central Line-Associated Bloodstream Infections [PDF - 2.2 MB]
    Compilation of guidance on topics including CLABSI surveillance, central line bundle, catheter maintenance, and prevention of infections among the pediatric population and in the renal setting, from the Association for Professionals in Infection Control and Epidemiology (APIC)
  • Assessment of Program for Prevention of Central Line-Associated Bloodstream Infections [PDF - 588 KB]
    Worksheet that provides a method to measure the level of implementation of an organization’s CLABSI prevention program, from the Pennsylvania Patient Safety Authority
  • Organizational Self-Assessment [PDF - 182 KB]
    Tool to conduct a self-assessment of organizational infrastructure related to CLABSI prevention with links to improvement tools, from The Joint Commission
  • Failure Modes Effect Analysis (FMEA) for CLABSI
    A suite of tools that assist in conducting assessments to identify highest risk failures leading to CLABSIs and the most important areas for process improvements, from the Utah Hospital Association
  • Eliminate Harm Across the Board [PDF - 329 KB]
    Checklist of CLABSI Prevention Strategies, from the Hospital Quality Institute, in partnership with the American Hospital Association, Partnership for Patients, and the Health Research & Educational Trust
  • CLABSI Event Report Template for Defect Analysis [DOC - 53 KB]
    Template report for the review of incident CLABSI, from the Hospital Quality Institute

Prevention Bundles

  • CDC Checklist for Prevention of CLABSI [PDF - 142 KB]
    Checklist based on 2011 CDC Guideline for Prevention of Intravascular Catheter-Associated Bloodstream Infections, from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention
  • How-to Guide: Prevent Central Line-Associated Bloodstream Infections
    Guide describing key evidence-based care components of a CLABSI Prevention Bundle, from the Institute for Healthcare Improvement (IHI). Log-in Required
  • CLABSI Prevention Toolkits & Resources
    Toolkit of CLABSI prevention resources, from the Armstrong Institute for Patient Safety and Quality at Johns Hopkins Medicine

Staff Training and Competency Assessments - Insertion

  • Certification for the Insertion of Central Venous Access Devices [PDF - 44 KB]
    Sample policy related to certification of graduate medical students to insert central lines, from Stony Brook University Hospital
  • Baystate Health Central Line Insertion Privileging for Attending Physicians [PDF - 47 KB]
    Sample policy related to central line insertion credentialing and privileging for physicians, from Baystate Medical Center
  • Staff Requirements for Placing PICC and Midline Vascular Access Devices
    Sample policy detailing requirements for staff who place PICC or midline vascular access devices, from University of Kentucky HealthCare
  • Vascular Catheter Insertion Checklist [PDF - 552 KB]
    Checklist to be completed by the inserter and assistant to document steps before, during, and after central line insertion, from BJC Healthcare
  • Central Line Insertion Standard Work and Safety Checklist[DOC - 65 KB]
    Checklist for standard work before, during, and after central line insertion procedure, from Kaiser Permanente
  • Johns Hopkins Hospital Central Line Insertion Checklist [PDF - 28 KB]
    A central line insertion checklist for the care team, from Johns Hopkins Health System
  • Central Line Insertion Care Team Checklist
    Checklist providing sequential critical steps that have shown to reduce infections; from the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ)
  • Videos in Clinical Medicine: Central Venous Catheterization
    Video demonstrating proper technique during the placement of an internal jugular central venous catheter, from the New England Journal of Medicine
  • Videos in Clinical Medicine: PICC Placement in the Neonate
    Video demonstrating proper technique during the placement of a peripherally inserted central catheter in a neonate, from the New England Journal of Medicine

Documentation of Insertion

  • Physician Procedure Note for Invasive Line Insertion [DOC - 55 KB]
    Sample procedure note for documentation of central line insertion, from HealthEast Care System and the Collaborative Healthcare-Associated Infection Network: Preventing HAIs in Minnesota
  • Central Line Insertion Procedure Note [PDF - 121 KB]
    Sample procedure note for documentation of central line insertion, from Roxborough Memorial Hospital and the Pennsylvania Patient Safety Authority
  • Vascular Access Procedure Note [PDF - 118 KB]
    Sample procedure note for documentation of central line insertion, from Beth Israel Medical Center

Staff Training and Competency Assessments - Maintenance

  • Central Line Dressing Change
    Video for staff education on central line dressing changes, from the Armstrong Institute for Patient Safety and Quality at Johns Hopkins Medicine
  • "Be a life SAVER" Online Educational Module
    Educational module on central line dressing changes providing interactive vignettes and test materials for use as a stand-alone educational module, from Utah Hospital Association
  • Vascular Access Device Quiz for the Prevention of CLABSI [DOC - 51 KB]
    Quiz designed to test healthcare personnel knowledge on CLABSI prevention techniques, from Johns Hopkins University, Quality and Safety Research Group
  • Clean It Like You Mean It [PDF - 1.8 MB]
    Overview and examples from the “Clean It Like You Mean It” campaign collaborative that uses evidence-based best practices and nurse education to reduce CLABSIs, prepared by Morgan Stanley Children’s Hospital at New York Presbyterian Hospital and posted by the American Association of Critical Care Nurses

Routine Audits

  • Central Line Insertion Practices (CLIP) Training Module [PDF - 161 KB]
    National Healthcare Safety Network (NSHN) training course on the collection of data about central line insertion practices, from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
  • Central Line Maintenance Audit Form
    Audit form to assess central line maintenance practices, from the Agency for Healthcare Quality and Promotion (AHRQ)
  • Central Line Maintenance Bundle [PDF - 144 KB]
    Checklist/audit form to assess central line maintenance practices, from IPRO, the Medicare Quality Improvement Organization for New York State
  • Dressing Integrity Observation Audit Form [PDF - 21 KB]
    Audit tool for central line dressings and appropriate changes of administration sets, from the University of Rochester
  • Nursing Practice Observation Audit Form[PDF - 21 KB]
    Audit tool to collect observations when nurses are changing central line dressings, from the University of Rochester
  • Central Line Outcome and Process Measures Worksheet [PDF - 361 KB]
    Worksheet to assist with calculation of outcome and process measures related to central line insertion, care, and maintenance, from the PennsylvaniaPatient Safety Authority
  • Bloodstream Infection Event, Device-associated Module [PDF - 587 KB]
    Surveillance guidance with the current National Healthcare Safety Network (NHSN) CLABSI definition (revised January 2016), from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
  • CLABSI Review Form [PDF - 235 KB]
    Fillable worksheet for the review of incident CLABSIs, from the University of Rochester
  • CLABSI Audit Tool [XLS - 152 KB]
    A downloadable Excel file that calculates compliance with guidelines for the maintenance of central lines, from IPRO, the Quality improvement Organization for the State of New York

Feedback for Providers

  • CLABSI Investigation Nurse Letter
    Letter Template to assist in investigation of breaches that may be associated with CLABSIs, from the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ)
  • Weeks Without CLABSI Banner
    Downloadable PowerPoint slide to inform staff of number of weeks since last CLABSI, from the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ)
  • Eliminate Harm Across the Board: Days Since Last CLABSI Checklist and Poster [PDF - 329 KB]
    Poster to inform staff of the days since the last CLABSI, including a list of prevention measures, from the Hospital Quality Institute
