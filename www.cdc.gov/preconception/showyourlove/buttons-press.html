Get Involved with Show Your Love Campaign

To help increase awareness, a number of resources have been created for partners. Some campaign materials target specific groups, including women who are planning a pregnancy, women who aren't planning a pregnancy in the near future, couples, and women who already have a family. Consider your own organization's goals and priorities when choosing from the materials below.

Link Your Website to Ours

The best way to help people in your community learn more about your organization and the Show Your Love campaign is to link the two together – literally – on the web site. We developed buttons that includes the campaign logo and photo along with the web address.

Button for women who want to become pregnant

To add the button, copy the code below the button and paste it into your web page.

Checklists

Women can use these checklists to set goals and make a health plan. They can also use the checklist to discuss their goals with a doctor or healthcare provider. The checklists come in two printable formats: 1) a version with five 8.5” x 11” pages that includes images and 2) a condensed, trifold 8.5” x 11” version without any images.
