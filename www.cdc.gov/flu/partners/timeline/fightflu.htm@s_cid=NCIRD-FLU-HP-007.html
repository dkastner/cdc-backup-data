Influenza (Flu)

CDC's #FightFlu Social Media Campaign

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

The #FightFlu social media campaign captures and shares flu vaccination activities. During or after getting a flu shot, share photos and videos of yourself (tag #FightFlu) on digital platforms (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube). CDC will post weekly updates to the campaign timeline. Need more information? Contact fluinbox@cdc.gov.
