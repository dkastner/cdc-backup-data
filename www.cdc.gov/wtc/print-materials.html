Print Materials

The WTC Health Program offers a variety of print materials developed for eligible responders and survivors. Materials are organized by theme. Map themed materials ask the audience “Were you there?” and shows the New York City map boundaries that are included in the Program. Portrait and Collage themed materials seek to make a personal connection and show that many different groups of people and occupations are part of the WTC Health Program.

Materials are available to download in two print formats. Choose Basic for electronic distribution or if you are printing on a standard office or home printer. Choose Press Ready if you are working with a professional printer. Press Ready files include bleeds and crop-marks.

Most print materials are available in English, and Spanish, Polish, and Chinese translations.
