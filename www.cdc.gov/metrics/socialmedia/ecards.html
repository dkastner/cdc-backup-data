2015 Annual Summary

Launch Date: July 2006

Analysis: Since 2007, there have been 224,002 total eCards sent and 714,610 eCards viewed. An eCard which is "SENT" refers to the number of times a visitor sent an eCard to another person. An eCard which is "VIEWED" refers to the number of times the recipient clicked on the eCard link in their email and opened/viewed the eCard itself.

The most viewed eCard is Flu Prevention-Health Professionals, with 74,253 views. The most frequently sent eCard is the 12 Ways to Health, which was sent over 12,037 times.
