2015 Annual Summary

Launch Date: July 2006

Analysis:

Since launch:

  • CDC RSS Feeds decreased 15.2% from the prior year. In 2015 they reached over 165 million.
  • The most popular CDC RSS feed is " RSS: CDC Facebook Feed_199" with 15,034,526 views
  • CDC Podcasts Downloads decreased 38.2% from the prior year. Podcasts were downloaded over 4.7 million times in 2015
  • The most popular Podcast download is "Podcast (t): Making Texas Restaurants Healthier for Children" with 56,438 downloads.
