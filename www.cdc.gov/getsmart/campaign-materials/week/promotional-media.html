Materials and References

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

CDC’s Get Smart: Know When Antibiotics Work program offers a number of materials and tools to help you learn about antibiotic resistance and appropriate antibiotic prescribing and use for common infections. Permission is not needed to print, copy, or distribute any materials from the Get Smart program.

  • Print Materials

    Get brochures, fact sheets, posters, and other materials to learn about using antibiotics wisely. Some are available in Spanish and customized for special groups…

  • Video and Audio Tools

    Listen to podcasts, review and watch videos on the role we all play in preventing antibiotic resistance...

  • Web Tools

    Encourage family and friends to “Get Smart” with buttons, badges, Health-e-cards, and web feature stories...

  • Social Media Messages

    Share the importance of appropriate prescribing and using antibiotics wisely through your social media channels...

  • Graphics

    Check out these images that help explain the growing concern about antibiotic resistance...

  • Press Materials

    Access a variety of resources and tools designed for the media...

  • Research Articles

    Read journal publications on antibiotic resistance, appropriate antibiotic prescribing and use, and other related issues...
