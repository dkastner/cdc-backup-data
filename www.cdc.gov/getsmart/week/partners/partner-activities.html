Antelope Valley Hospital

Event:  In preparation for Get Smart Week, the Antelope Valley Hospital Pharmacy Department distributed brochures and flyers at the Annual Benefits Fair on November 4.  

To engage patients, providers and the public during Get Smart Week, Antelope Valley Hospital will:

  • Send letters to community and hospital physicians emphasizing the importance of proper antimicrobial prescribing – these letters will be accompanied by setting-specific and Get Smart Week fact sheets
  • Place educational posters in designated areas for hospital physicians throughout the week
  • Change all hospital computers’ screen savers to display CDC’s get smart fact sheets about appropriate antibiotics use in hospital settings.
  • Print and display large-size CDC posters and fact sheets in public areas, such as hospital lobbies and entrances.
  • Issue a press release about Get Smart Week

The Pharmacy department will host a table distributing brochures about cold and flu (for general population and for parents), fact sheets for parents of both younger and older children, and fact sheets about the global threat of antibiotic resistance. The department will also administer the Get Smart Antibiotics Quiz – participants who get all 8 questions right will be entered into a raffle for prizes!

Finally, the Antimicrobial Stewardship team will visit three neighboring nursing homes. The team will distribute a print version of CDC’s toolkit about implementing an antimicrobial stewardship program in the nursing home setting as well as a flyer about the latest warnings about the FDA warnings regarding Fluoroquinolones.

Date/Time: November 14 – 20

Connect with Antelope Valley Hospital on Facebook and Twitter.
