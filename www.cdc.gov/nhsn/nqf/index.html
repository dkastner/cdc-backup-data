NHSN Measures Endorsed by National Quality Forum (NQF)

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

The National Quality Forum (NQF) is a not-for-profit, nonpartisan, membership-based organization that works to catalyze improvements in healthcare. NQF endorsement is the gold standard for healthcare quality. NQF-endorsed measures are evidence-based and valid, and in tandem with the delivery of care and payment reform.
