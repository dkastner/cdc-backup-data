Partnership

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Sign up to become a partner of the Clean Hands Count campaign. We want to hear from you. Please tell us:

  • Who do your clean hands count for?
  • What are you doing to promote clean hands in healthcare?
  • How are you using Clean Hands Count materials?
  • Do you have a good way to ask healthcare providers to clean their hands?

Use the form below to share your story with CDC and it may be featured on CDC’s Safe Healthcare Blog. Or just sign up to be counted as a partner. Either way, we’re glad you’re interested in hand hygiene!

Clean Hands Count campaign materials can be found on the Promotional Materials and Media and Social Media pages.
