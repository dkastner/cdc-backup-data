Field Epidemiology Training Program: Scientific Publications

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

The Field Epidemiology Training Program (FETP) establishes and strengthens a trained workforce disease detectives around the world. Our publications reflect the diversity of public health issues from emerging infectious diseases to noncommunicable diseases that create an enormous burden on underequipped public health systems. The work cited below addresses disease-specific outbreaks and emergencies, surveillance and laboratory science, and cross-cutting priorities related to disease threats. These publications show the diversity and strong scientific foundations of CDC’s public health work.
