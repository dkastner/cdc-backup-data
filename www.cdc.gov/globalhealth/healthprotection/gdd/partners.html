Global Disease Detection Program: Partners

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

On this Page

  • GDD Center Partners
  • United States Government Agencies
  • Universities
  • Non-Governmental Organizations

The Global Disease Detection (GDD) program collaborates closely with other Centers and programs at CDC to accomplish its mission. This collaboration allows GDD to draw upon expertise from across the agency. GDD also works closely with a variety of in-country partners including ministries of health, academic institutions, other U.S. government programs, and international and non-government organizations. These partners contribute funding, expertise, and other resources towards GDD projects and goals. These partners include:
