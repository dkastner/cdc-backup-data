Global Health

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

CDC has been working around the world for more than 60 years.

The reason is simple: in today’s interconnected world, a disease threat anywhere is a disease threat everywhere. We help detect and control outbreaks at their source so that Americans can live healthier, more stable, and productive lives.

Learn more about what CDC is doing > (http://www.cdc.gov/globalhealth/what/default.htm)
