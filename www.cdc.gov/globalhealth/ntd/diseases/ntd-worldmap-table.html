*Global Overlap of six of the common NTDs. Specifically guinea worm disease, lymphatic filariasis, onchocerciasis, schistosomiasis, soil-transmitted helminths, trachoma.

**Soil-transmitted helminth infections can be caused by three different worms, all treated the same way.
