Where We Work

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

At the heart of our highly capable and committed global health staff are the more than 342 assignees in 50 plus countries, including more than 40 staff detailed to international organizations. They are supported by the expertise and efforts of approximately 1,368 locally employed staff, contract staff, fellows and hundreds of headquarters staff who travel overseas each year to provide temporary technical assistance.
