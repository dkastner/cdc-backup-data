CDC Contract Funding Allocations

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

In FY 2015, OFR processed 11,459 contract actions that totaled over $5 billion. The table below shows how CDC contracts are allocated among CDC’s Centers, Institutes, and Offices (CIOs). The National Institute of Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) produced the highest number of contract actions; however, the majority of contract spending was through the National Center for Immunization and Respiratory Diseases (NCIRD), primarily for the purchase of vaccines.
