  • Vital Signs  Town Hall Teleconferences, which offer recent data and calls to action following the release of CDC's monthly Vital Signs  reports

  • National Public Health Improvement Initiative (NPHII), a CDC-funded project that supported health departments to improve the delivery and impact of public health services
