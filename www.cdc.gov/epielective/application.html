Application Information

To be eligible for the Epi Elective Program, you must be:

  • A U.S. citizen, permanent resident, or foreign national with proper student visa
  • A 3rd year medical or veterinary student when applying
  • Available for 6-8 weeks during your 4th year
  • Enrolled in a school accredited by one of the following:
    • Liaison Committee on Medical Education
    • American Osteopathic Association
    • American Veterinary Medical Association

Step 1:  Prepare your application

  • Review eligibility
  • Update your CV
  • Write your personal statement
  • Obtain a signed Academic Endorsement [PDF - 1 page] from the student affairs office at your medical or veterinary school
  • Provide your two recommendation writers with the Instructions for Letters of Recommendation [PDF - 2 pages] .

Step 2: Submit your application

  • Complete the Online Application by the deadline
  • Confirm that your recommendation writers have submitted your letters by the deadline

Application Materials must be submitted by the deadline.

Letters of Recommendation

  • Two letters of recommendation are required
  • Select individuals who know you professionally and with whom you have recently worked
  • At least 1 letter must be from a faculty member of your school
  • Provide recommenders with a copy of the Instructions for Letters of Recommendation [PDF - 2 pages]

Academic Endorsement

  • Download the Academic Endorsement [PDF - 1 page]
  • Have the form completed and signed by the student affairs office at your medical or veterinary school
  • Upload to the Epidemiology Elective Program online application by the deadline
