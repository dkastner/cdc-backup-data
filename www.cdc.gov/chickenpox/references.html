Vaccine-related Resources

  • Prevention of Varicella—Recommendations of the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices 2007 June
    Recommendations adopted in 2005 and 2006 include implementation of a routine 2-dose varicella vaccination program for children; a second dose catch-up varicella vaccination for children, adolescents, and adults who previously had received 1 dose; and routine vaccination of all healthy persons older than 13 years and without evidence of immunity.
  • Use of Combination Measles, Mumps, Rubella, and Varicella Vaccine—Recommendations of the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices 2010 May
    Recommendations adopted in 2009 regarding use of MMRV vaccine for the first and second doses and identification of a personal or family (i.e., sibling or parent) history of seizure as a precaution for use of MMRV vaccine.
