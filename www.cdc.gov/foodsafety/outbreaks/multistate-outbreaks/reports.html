MMWR Reports on Foodborne Illness and Outbreaks

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

 

The Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report (MMWR) series is prepared by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. The MMWR series is the agency’s primary vehicle for scientific publication of timely, reliable, authoritative, accurate, objective, and useful public health information and recommendations.

This page contains a listing of MMWR publications on foodborne and other enteric (gastrointestinal) disease outbreak investigations and surveillance and trends.
