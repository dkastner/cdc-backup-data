Stats of the States

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

Click on map or state name for individual state data:

  • Percent of Babies Born to Unmarried Mothers, by State
  • Percent of Births by Cesarean Delivery, by State - The percentage of all live births that were cesarean deliveries.
  • Percentage of Births Born Preterm, by State - Babies born prior to 37 weeks of pregnancy (gestation).
  • Teen Birth Rate by State - The number of births per 1,000 teenagers ages 15-19 in each state.
  • Percentage Born Low Birthweight by State - Babies born weighing less than 2,500 grams or 5 lbs. 8oz.
  • Leading Causes of Death, by State, 2014 [PDF - 302 MB] - This comprehensive table lists the 15 leading causes of death in each state, and the District of Columbia.
  • Heart Disease Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from diseases of the heart.
  • Cancer Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from malignant neoplasms.
  • Chronic Lower Respiratory Disease Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from various lung diseases including asthma, chronic bronchitis, and emphysema.
  • Accident Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from unintentional injuries/accidents.
  • Stroke Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from cerebrovascular disease.
  • Alzheimer's Disease Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from Alzheimer's disease.
  • Diabetes Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from diabetes mellitus.
  • Influenza and Pneumonia Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from flu and pneumonia.
  • Kidney Disease Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from nephritis, nephrotic syndrome and nephrosis.
  • Suicide Mortality by State - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from suicide
  • Death Rates for Drug Poisoning, by State of Residence: United States - The latest state-based age-adjusted death rates for fatal drug poisonings.
  • Death Rates from Firearm Injuries: United States - The latest state-based age-adjusted death rates for firearm-related fatalities.
  • Death Rates from Homicide: United States - The latest state-based age-adjusted death rates for assault.
  • Death Rates from Septicemia: United States - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from blood poisoning
  • Death Rates from Chronic Liver Disease/Cirrhosis: United States - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from chronic liver diseases
  • Death Rates from Hypertension: United States - Number of deaths and age-adjusted death rates from hypertensive disease.
  • Infant Mortality Rates by State - The number of deaths to infants under one year of age per 1,000 live births.
  • Percentage of People of All Ages Without Health Insurance Coverage: United States
  • Marriage Rates by State, 2015 [PDF - 239 KB] - The number of marriages per 1,000 total population.
  • Divorce Rates by State, 2015 [PDF - 239 KB] - The number of divorces per 1,000 total population. An analysis of 44 states.
  • FastStats - Quick facts on the number of births and deaths in each state, along with key links to other references.

 

* For more information or to call 301-458-4800.
