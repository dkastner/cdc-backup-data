Naming convention for Summary Health Statistics Tables:

  • "ALC-#", "BW-#", "PA-#", "SLP-#", "TOB-#" tables are based on U.S. Adults
  • “A-#” tables are based on U.S. adults
  • “C-#” tables are based on U.S. children
  • “P-#” tables are based on the U.S. population
  • “a” worksheets contain age-adjusted percentages or rates
  • “b” worksheets contain frequencies
  • “c” worksheets contain crude (unadjusted) percentages or rates
