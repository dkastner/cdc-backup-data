Series 16. Compilations of Advance Data From Vital and Health Statistics

Recommend on Facebook Tweet ShareCompartir

These reports provide early release of data from the health and demographic surveys of the National Center for Health Statistics. Many of these releases are followed by detailed reports in the Vital and Health Statistics Series.

We are no longer posting these reports on the Internet.

Persons with disabilities who experience problems accessing PDF files should contact nchsed@cdc.gov or call 301-458-4688.

Citation as to source is appreciated, when quotations are used in publications.
