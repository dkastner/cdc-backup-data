Provisional Tables On Births, Marriages, Divorces, and Deaths 2001-2003

Volume 52, No. 1 to 8

Detailed tables prepared by the Division of Vital Statistics, National Center for Health Statistics, include numbers and rates for births, deaths (including infant deaths), marriages, and divorces by State. For discussion of the nature, sources, and limitations of provisional data from the National Vital Statistics System, see the Technical notes of the associated monthly report. Figures have been revised to include updates received from the States.

Notice! These detailed tables are included in the provisional data National Vital Statistics Reports (NVSRs) effective with Volume 52, Number 11.
